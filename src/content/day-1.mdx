## Reminder: What is AI Fluency?

AI Fluency is the capability to translate intentions into reliable AI outcomes. It blends problem framing, prompt craft, and system sense. At its core is an intuitive grasp of model behavior—how inputs map to outputs—and the ability to steer that behavior with structure and constraints.

Practical strategies you can start using now:

- Scaffold your prompts with roles, constraints, and verification steps for more predictable results.
- Iterate deliberately: change one variable at a time and keep a short log of prompt -> outcome pairs.

<Callout>
  <strong className="font-serif text-black">Key idea:</strong> Treat the model as a <Highlight>reasoning partner</Highlight>, not an oracle. Make uncertainty explicit and verify.
</Callout>
